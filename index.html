“One Intune-governed workspace. Desktops right-sized. Apps delivered and patched. Day one.”

The Offer

  • One workspace, governed by Intune — desktops and apps delivered together, not separate.
  • One motion — provisioned, right-sized, patched, and policy-compliant at the same time.
  • Built on Microsoft — Azure-native, Intune-driven, Marketplace/MACC eligible.

Why Change

  1. Manual app delivery stalls projects — Cloud PCs/AVD spin up in minutes, but apps take weeks to package, patch, and approve, leaving users waiting.
  2. Overprovisioned infra wastes money — idle pools, image drift, and manual scaling push Azure spend up to 55% higher than necessary.
  3. Governance is fragmented — desktops in one system, apps in another; without unification under Intune, compliance slips and innovation slows.

Why Now

  1. Release cycles are accelerating — OS, SaaS, and AI updates drop constantly; manual processes can’t keep pace.
  2. Zero-day & compliance pressure — every day of patch delay is a measurable risk window for attackers and auditors.
  3. Do more with less — IT headcount is flat, Azure bills are rising; automation is the only lever left.

Why Better Together

  1. Complete the workspace — Nerdio (desktops) + Recast (apps) under Intune governance = a secure, productive Cloud PC from day one.
  2. Prove ROI in days, not months — < 1 hr to live desktops + apps; 5–10 hrs/app packaging avoided; >95% patch compliance; 30–40% lower infra/ops spend (with up to 55% Azure savings from autoscale).
  3. Migration-safe, MSP-ready — Smart Icons for zero-disruption cutover; parallel tracks enable value while migrations build; repeatable offer makes it easy for MSPs to roll out across tenants.

Use Cases

  • Windows 365 / AVD rollout — deliver desktops + apps + policy together via Intune.
  • SCCM → Intune modernization — keep SCCM-style sophistication while shifting to modern management.
  • Zero-day compliance — coordinate desktop + app patching to shrink exposure windows.
  • M&A / rapid onboarding — governed desktops + apps in <1 hr.
  • MSP multi-tenant enablement — repeatable “workspace in a box” offer that scales across customers.
